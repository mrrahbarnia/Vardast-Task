## Overview
The Smart Blood Pressure Monitor is designed for home use and can connect to your phone via Bluetooth.

## Instructions
1. Wrap the cuff around your upper arm.
2. Press the start button to begin measurement.
3. The results will appear on the screen and sync with the mobile app.

## Troubleshooting
If the device fails to connect, ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone and the monitor has sufficient battery.
